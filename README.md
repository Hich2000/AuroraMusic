# Aurora music
Active development ongoing.\
Disclaimer: This is a music app meant for local play, not a spotify-like platform.

## What is Aurora music?
Aurora Music is a personal project to create a music app specially tailored to my own needs. It started with a simple thought: "I hate making playlist". I love music, but I hate the concept of playlists. I know this sounds really weird, but let me explain.

The music I listen varies wildly from day to day, mood to mood, activity to activity, etc etc. You get it. So why not just make playlists for every activity, or mood or whatever? Because a playlist is too static for my use case. Lets take coding for example. Some days I might want to relax and listen to some smooth jazz, and just get in a groove. On other days I might want to listen to something more upbeat and put on some Shimokita-kei. Some days I might want to combine some of the songs between the two and have it jump between genres, and on the day after I might want to add another style/vibe/genre to the mix.

Are you starting to see the problem? If I were to make playlists, I would end up with way too many of them. My music taste is also always evolving, I love looking into new genres or artist and adding them to my repertoire. So whenever I find something new, my playlist count would grow exponentially. So instead of resigning myself to my fate, I decided to tackle my rather strange and unique predicament. My idea? Tags.

Instead of creating playlists, you can instead create a tag. The tag is just a simple piece of text to describe something. Wether that be a language, a specific activity, or a mood, you name it. You can then add these tags to songs from your music list, and you can add as many as you want to be as specific as possible. You can then have every song with a specific tag added to the queue to listen to.

"Wait a second, how is this any different from regular playlists?"

Good question, the difference here is that you can select mulitple tags and the app will combine them into a single queue. You can even exclude tags so that songs that have that tag are not included in the queue. This allows you to easily and quickly tailor your mood for every occasion.

## Current features
- Functional mediaplayer
- Creating tags
- Creating categories for tags
- Assigning tags to songs
- Selecting folders to scan for music files
- Queue creation through selected tags
- Light mode and dark mode options
